Employee count — hunt deer, not elephants

Companies tab
Where: Type employee count in the search filters box.
Min
51
Max
1000
🏃 Rabbits, deer, elephants
  • GCs: yours from 51 staff up — ICP 3 (51–1,000) and ICP 2 (1,001+).
  • Subs, service providers & utility: yours only at ICP 2 (1,001+ staff) — set the min to 1,001 when hunting those.
  • Deer: ICP 3 GCs (51–1,000) move fastest — live here.
  • Elephants (1,001+): great, but 60–180 days — work them in the background.
⚡ Match the platform to the company type before you call
  • GCs → ConstructionWire + PanteraTools/QuestCDN.
  • Specialty subs → QuestCDN + ConstructionWire.
  • Distributors → Construction Monitor + ConstructionWire.
🚫 Skip the revenue filter entirely
  • Public revenue data is garbage.
  • I've seen a $20M company listed at $900K — and a $2M company listed at $33M.
  • Employee count is your size filter. Period.

Scoops & Intent — your call triggers
Contacts + Companies

    
  

  

    
Where: Type scoops in the search filters box.

    
    
      
  • Scoops = anything that would show up in a news article or press release about a company.
    • 
      
  • Perfect first-call reasons.
    • 
    

    
Search these:

    
Scoop types to try
Hiring Plans
New Offices
Expansions
Partnerships
Leadership Changes

    
    
      
  • Hiring — a GC or sub hiring estimators and BD people is investing in growth. Same trigger works for you.
    • 
      
  • New offices / expansions / partnerships — a contractor expanding into your states is a fresh relationship window. Leadership changes — a new VP of Pre-Con is new ears with no incumbent loyalty.
    • 
      
  • On any company page, click Scoops to see theirs. Verify with a quick Google ("[company] estimator jobs").
    • 
      
  • In your Zoominfo training, when you hear the word scoops — lean in. Ask which scoops fit contractors.
    • 
    

    
Bonus — Intent: Type intent → pick topics relevant to your platforms. It shows companies actively researching those topics right now.
